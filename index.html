Why it matters

The ozone layer protects life by absorbing harmful UV‑B/UV‑C radiation. Faster restoration means fewer skin cancers, safer ecosystems, and better crop yields.

How it works

Our generator uses onboard power to create “good ozone” and inject it precisely along airline routes, augmenting natural O3 formation in the lower stratosphere.

Path to scale

Deploy across tens of thousands of aircraft via retrofit and line‑fit partnerships. Instrument with sensing to monitor local O3 and ensure safety.

Pilot program

We’re preparing a limited pilot with partner airlines to validate efficiency, safety, and operations. If your organization is interested in collaborating, join the waitlist and note “pilot.”

Safety first

Generation is “delicate by design,” targeting low‑ppm mixing and strict dispersion away from the cabin and ground‑level air. Controls, logging, and geofenced flight envelopes back this approach.
